Margie’s Wordle

How To Play

Guess the word in 6 tries.

  • Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word. British English spellings only — think fibre, not fiber.
  • The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

You can play up to 5 games a day — after you finish a game, choose Play again for a new word. A fresh set of 5 unlocks at midnight.

Stuck? The 💡 button reveals a letter that’s in the word and that you haven’t tried yet. Using it marks the game with a 💡 in your results — so glory is reserved for unaided wins. When a game ends, the meaning of the word is shown.
